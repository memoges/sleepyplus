Wie funktioniert Sleepy+?

Jede App, die Audio auf deinem iPhone abspielt – Spotify, Apple Music, Podcasts, Audible, YouTube und mehr.

Ja! 7 Tage kostenlos mit allen Premium-Funktionen. Das Abo verlängert sich danach automatisch, kann aber jederzeit vorher gekündigt werden.

FAQ

How does Sleepy+ work?

Start your music, audiobook, or podcast. Open Sleepy+, set your sleep timer and alarm, and tap the sleep button. Your audio stops after the set time, and the alarm wakes you up the next morning.

Which audio apps are supported?

Any app that plays audio on your iPhone – Spotify, Apple Music, Podcasts, Audible, YouTube, and more.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Sleepy+ → Cancel. Premium access remains until the end of the paid period.

Can I try it for free?

Yes! 7-day free trial with all premium features. The subscription renews automatically, but you can cancel anytime.

How do I get a refund?
